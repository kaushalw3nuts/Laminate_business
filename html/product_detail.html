Extruded Acrylic Sheets

Product details:

Extruded Acrylic Sheets are a versatile and cost-effective solution for a wide range of applications. Known for their excellent clarity, durability, and ease of fabrication, extruded acrylic sheets are a popular choice in industries such as signage, displays, and glazing.

Technical Specifications:
  • Material : Extruded Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA)
  • Density : 1.19 g/cm³
  • Service Temperature : -40°C to +80°C
  • UV Resistance : High
  • Pure Acrylic
  • Anti Scratch Hardness
  • Termite & Border FREE
